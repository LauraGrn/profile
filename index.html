My favourite food spots in Amsterdam so far at the moment

Coffee & Coconuts

A funky and fresh place to enjoy the buzzing energy of the city. The food is high quality and made together with local passionate people who love what they brew, bake or shake. If you love coconuts, you must come here!

Restaurant en Kwekerij De Kas

A unique restaurant located in a set of greenhouses which date back to 1926 with the freshest local ingredients grown on location including all herbs and vegetables in the set course meals.

The Seafood Bar

The Seafood Bar is a modern restaurant that offers a wide array of the freshest seafood. Each dish is beautifully prepared with great passion and care. Dinner here is an amazing experience in a casual and relaxing setting.
